Evan Dong

I'm a 3rd-year PhD student in Computer Science at Cornell University, advised by Nikhil Garg and Angelina Wang. My work focuses broadly on responsible machine learning, with particular emphasis on technical interventions informed by social theory. I am affiliated with Cornell's Artificial Intelligence, Policy, and Practice initiative and with the Digital Life Initiative as a Doctoral Fellow. I am happy to chat about research, graduate school, and the labor movement. You can contact me at edong [at] cs.cornell.edu.

For graduate students with workplace concerns, questions, or interest in the Cornell graduate student union, also known as CGSU-UE Local 300, please email rls@cgsu.org or fill out the form linked here.
